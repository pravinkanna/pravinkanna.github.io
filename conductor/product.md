# Initial Concept
A multi-functional personal portfolio designed to showcase professional milestones and technical skills, while also serving as a platform for technical blogging and a suite of lightweight developer utilities.

# Product Guide

## Vision
To build a comprehensive personal portfolio that effectively showcases professional experience and technical expertise to recruiters, hiring managers, and developer peers. The site will serve as a dual-purpose platform: a professional resume and a utility hub hosting technical blogs and developer tools.

## Target Audience
- **Recruiters & Hiring Managers:** Looking for a clear, chronological history of work and a quick assessment of technical skills.
- **Developers & Technical Peers:** Interested in technical blog content, open-source projects, and handy developer tools.

## Core Features
- **Professional Timeline:** A chronological display of career history and milestones.
- **Skills Matrix:** A categorized presentation of technical skills and technologies.
- **Blog Section:** A dedicated area at `/blogs` for sharing technical insights and articles.
- **Developer Tools:** A suite of simple browser-based tools (e.g., GiB to MiB calculator) hosted under `/tools/tool_name`.
